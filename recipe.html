Cookin' Da Eggs (Scrambled!)

Ingredients

  • 2–3 fresh eggs (room temperature works best!)
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black pepper (freshly ground is great!)
  • Optional: chopped herbs (like parsley or chives)
  • Optional: grated cheese (cheddar or Parmesan)
  • Optional: toast or bread for serving

Instructions

  1. **Crack** 2–3 eggs into a bowl.
  2. Add a pinch of salt and pepper.
  3. **Whisk** well with a fork or small whisk until the yolks and whites are fully combined and slightly frothy.
  4. **Heat** a non-stick pan over **low to medium-low** heat. Add the butter or oil.
  5. Pour in the egg mixture. Let it set for about 30 seconds without touching.
  6. Gently use a spatula to push the eggs from the edges toward the center, creating soft curds. Tilt the pan to let uncooked egg flow underneath.
  7. Continue stirring gently for 1–2 minutes. Cook until soft, slightly creamy, and still slightly wet (they continue cooking on the plate!).
  8. **Serve hot** immediately with toast, herbs, or toppings of your choice.
Okie, enjoy your perfect, creamy scrambled eggs! :)
